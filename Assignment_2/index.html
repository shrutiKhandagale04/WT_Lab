About Me

My fascination with computers ignited at the tender age of 5 when I first encountered MS Paint on a damaged PC. Curiosity led me to question the mysterious "motherboard" and thus began my journey into the realm of technology. This early interest laid the foundation for my future endeavors.

Moving to different cities and schools broadened my horizons. In Chandrapur, at Narayana Vidyalam, my teenage years unfolded, and my passion for computers deepened. The subsequent relocation to Yeola in Nashik marked the beginning of my commute to Sanjivani Academy, where I spent two years of high school and one year pursuing a diploma in computer technology at K.B.P Polytechnic Kopargaon.

The pivotal moment during the pandemic was the creation of a sponsored website for Phinix Crane dealers during the last semester of my diploma. This hands-on experience not only showcased my technical skills but also demonstrated the real-world applications of my knowledge.

Opting for Sanjivani College of Engineering for my Information Technology course was a strategic choice. Being an autonomous and NAAC-accredited A-grade college, it provided the ideal environment for fostering innovation and academic excellence.

Throughout my journey, I've actively engaged in various projects, guided by experienced faculty members. These projects have not only honed my technical skills but have also instilled in me a problem-solving mindset, a crucial aspect in the ever-evolving field of technology.

My love for reading, which began in the 5th grade, has been a constant companion. It has enhanced my analytical thinking and broadened my perspective. This intellectual curiosity, coupled with my technical acumen, positions me as a valuable contributor to technological development.

In conclusion, my autobiography reflects a journey marked by curiosity, adaptability, and a passion for technology. As I continue my academic pursuits in Information Technology, I am poised to contribute meaningfully to the global technological landscape, and specifically, I see my experiences and skills as assets that could contribute to technological development.
